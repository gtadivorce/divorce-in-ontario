Divorce in Ontario: Filing Checklist

  • ✅ You or your spouse must have lived in Ontario for at least 12 months.
  • ✅ You must have valid grounds for divorce (usually separation for 1 year).
  • ✅ Gather documents: Marriage certificate, separation agreement, ID.
  • ✅ Choose the correct court (Superior Court of Justice).
  • ✅ Fill out and file the required forms:
    • - Form 8: Application (General or Simple)
    • - Form 36: Affidavit for Divorce
    • - Form 25A: Divorce Order
  • ✅ Pay the court filing fee (~$632 CAD).
  • ✅ Serve the application to your spouse (unless it's a joint application).
  • ✅ Wait for a response or the required time to pass.
  • ✅ Attend court, if necessary.
  • ✅ Receive your Divorce Order and Certificate of Divorce.

⚠️ This checklist is for informational purposes only and does not constitute legal advice. For official guidance, visit the Ontario government divorce page.
